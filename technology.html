Explore Our Data Layers

Dive deeper into the comprehensive data sets that power our platform. Scroll down to see how each layer provides unique insights into development landscapes.

1. Foundation: Base Map

Our base map provides the essential geographical context, displaying political boundaries, major cities, and foundational land features.

2. Demographics: Population Density

Understand human distribution with our population density layer, highlighting both densely populated urban centers and sparsely inhabited rural areas.

3. Connectivity: Infrastructure

Access crucial infrastructure data, including roads, schools, hospitals, and clinics, vital for assessing accessibility and development needs.

4. Environment: Climate Vulnerability

Identify regions most susceptible to climate change impacts like droughts, floods, and sea-level rise, informing climate resilience initiatives.

5. Impact: Project Locations

See real-world development projects from our partners overlaid on the map, providing a direct visualization of interventions and their geographical reach.
